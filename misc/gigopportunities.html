Free Promo Video

Acoustic Acts - Tynemouth All Day Mini-Festival

On 12th and 13th August we will be putting on a live music event at the coast to showcase new musicians. If you do originals (and some covers) and are looking for a good gig to display what you do send us a link to your music. You will be required to perform for 45 minutes. Time slots are between 2 and 9pm on both days. Every act will be given a cash payment, with PA being provided too.

The ideal musician should have some good original songs in their set, and we are open to guitar vocalists, piano vocalists, duos, trios and anyone who does something a bit different.
