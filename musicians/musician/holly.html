Holly


Holly is a guitar vocalist who grew up in the moors of the Durham Dales. Now based in Newcastle, she can liven up a venue with her acoustic covers of contemporary and classic hits, or sing her way to your heart with some gentle love songs. Holly is also an emerging singer-songwriter, bringing out her debut EP, ILEX, of original acoustic songs this summer
